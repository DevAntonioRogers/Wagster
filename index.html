What Is Wagster?

We exist because we believe in a world where no pet is euthanized for financial reasons. We are a group of passionate animal lovers. And we know how easy it is to be down on your luck, to fall through the cracks, to have to choose between your pet and paying your bills. So we set out on a mission to change the way pet safety nets work. To solve this problem, we had to innovate. Wagster is a revolutionary, not-for-profit crowdfunding model.

'I can't take care of you.'

Wagster exists because too many pet owners have had this tragic experience. And while everyone's path to this moment is different, the experience is the same: You love your dog or cat dearly, but you're faced with the realization that your pet needs a medical procedure that you can't afford. Reality hits like a ton of bricks. This innocent companion, who looks so lovingly at you, needs something you can't provide.
